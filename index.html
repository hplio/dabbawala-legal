02 — How We Use Your Information

Why We Process Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing your D DabbaWala account and subscription
  • Processing and delivering your tiffin orders to the correct address
  • Verifying payments through uploaded screenshots and activating your subscription
  • Sending order confirmations, delivery updates, and service notifications
  • Coordinating between home users, office users, and delivery partners
  • Resolving disputes, providing customer support, and improving our service
  • Complying with applicable laws and preventing fraud
We do not use your data for advertising purposes, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties under any circumstances.
03 — Sharing of Information

Third Parties We Work With

To provide our service, we share limited data with trusted third-party service providers:

  • Maps & Location Services — We use Google Maps to display delivery routes and address lookup. Your location is shared with Google solely for this purpose and governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics — We may use Firebase Analytics or similar tools to understand app usage patterns. This data is anonymized and aggregated.
  • Payment Processing — Payment verification details (screenshots) are handled internally by our admin team. We do not integrate with third-party payment gateways that store your card details.
  • Cloud Storage & Backend — Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

We require all third parties to maintain appropriate security standards and to use your data only for the specified purpose.

04 — Data Storage & Security

How We Protect Your Data

We take the security of your personal information seriously. D DabbaWala implements the following safeguards:

  • Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections at all times
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely using industry-standard practices
  • Access to user data in our backend systems is restricted to authorized personnel
  • Payment screenshots are stored securely and accessible only to verified admin accounts

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

While we implement strong technical measures, no internet-based service can guarantee 100% security. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your account credentials confidential.

📧 Send your deletion request to: bytenovawork@gmail.com
